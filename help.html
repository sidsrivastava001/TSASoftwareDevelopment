FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

1. How do I get to the dashboard?
First you have to make an account and then click the "Go to Dashboard" button in the top right corner of the home page. If you already have an account, you can click the same button and it will take you to the login screen and then the dashboard

2. Can I use this website if I am in college and looking to transfer or go to graduate school?
Yes, this website is for anyone who is planning to further their education at a college or university
